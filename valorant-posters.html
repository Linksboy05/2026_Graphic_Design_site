POSTER SERIES

A series of Valorant poster designs created to communicate character energy. The visuals blend bold type, strong compositions, and iconic agent details for a distinct Agent-focused look.

Role

Poster concept, layout, character art, visual direction

Tools

Photoshop, Illustrator

Deliverables

PROJECT OVERVIEW

BACK TO HOME →

Objective

Develop a striking poster series for Valorant that highlights key agents and supports a competitive event style. The work was designed to feel immersive, sharp, and consistent across printed and digital formats.

Approach

I created bold compositions, strong typography, and agent-focused imagery by combining character details with the background design based off the game's aesthetic and character design. Each poster pairs vibrant color accents with clean layout structure for visual impact.

Impact

The series presents a cohesive collection of promotional visuals ideal for esports campaigns, team branding, and fan engagement. It helps communicate the energy of each agent while supporting a polished, professional look.
