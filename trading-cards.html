TRADING CARDS

TRADING CARDS
DESIGN SYSTEM

Custom trading card created for esports players and collectible brands. The project combines bold graphic identity, polished mockups, and print-ready assets designed for both digital presentation and real-world distribution.

Role

Brand design, layout systems, mockup development

Tools

Illustrator, Photoshop, Affinity

Deliverables

Trading card templates, player artwork, brand assets

PROJECT OVERVIEW

BACK TO HOME →

Objective

Creating a trading card concept with a strong visual identity that works across digital presentation, event displays, and printed cards. The cards were designed to feel premium, energetic, and easy to adapt for different players and sponsors.

Approach

The project began with a visual system built around bold typography, electric gradients, and structured layouts. Each card features a prominent profile treatment, layered graphic accents, and branding cues that tie back to the esports narrative.

Impact

These card project creates a cohesive collectible experience while delivering polished assets for portfolio presentation, brand storytelling, and promotional use across social media and print.
